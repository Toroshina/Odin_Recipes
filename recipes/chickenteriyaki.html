Mac and Cheese

A quick, easy, and delicious chicken teriyaki recipe. This great for when you're busy and don't have the time to prep a full blown family meal.

  • 1 lb Chicken Thighs
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Finely chopped Green Onion
  • Toasted Seasame Seeds
  • 1 Cup of White Rice
  1. Place the chicken thighs in a container and add in the teriyaki sauce, marinate for 1 hour.
  2. Wash your white rice in a sieve until the water runs clear.
  3. Place your washed rice in a pot with 2 cups of water and bring to boil. Once it reaches a boil reduce the heat to a simmer and place lid on the pot (you might to need to have a small air gap).
  4. Grill your chicken thighs until cooked through, let rest for 5 min, slice into strips once rested.
  5. Plate the rice and chicken thighs on top of the rice. Lightly sprinkle on the seasame seeds and add on the green onion for garnish.
