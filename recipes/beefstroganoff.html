Beef Stroganoff

Creamy and savory Beef Stroganoff your family will love

This delicious beef stroganoff recipe is easy to prep and make in an instapot or a dutch oven. The added touch of red wine to deglaze the pan will elevate this dish to new heights.

  • Cubed stew meat (1lb)
  • Mushrooms (0.5lb)
  • Red cooking wine
  • Beef bouillon
  • One Onion
  • One glove of Garlic finely chopped
  • Sour Cream (to taste)
  • Heavy Cream (1/2 cup)
  • Green Onion (finely chopped)
  • EVOO
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Bring your dutch oven to a medium-high heat.
  2. While the dutch oven is coming up to temperature salt and pepper all the stew meat.
  3. Once the dutch oven is up to temperature add in the EVOO followed by the stew meat.
  4. Brown the stew meat on all sides and remove from the pan
  5. While the meat is browning chop one yellow or white onion
  6. Add in the chopped onion to the dutch oven and let sweat
  7. Add in the chopped mushrooms and saute for 3 min
  8. Add in the garlic once the onions become translucent and saute for 1 min
  9. Deglaze the pan with a splash of red cooking wine
  10. Add the browned stew meat, 1 can of beef broth, and 1 tbsp of beef bouillon and bring to a boil then reduce to simmer once boiling
  11. Cook until broth has reduced if not you can add a cornstartch slurry
  12. 10 min before serving have a pot of water boiling and add in the egg noodles
  13. Once ready to serve add the 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  14. Plate with the a dallop of sour cream and finely chopped green onions on top and serve
