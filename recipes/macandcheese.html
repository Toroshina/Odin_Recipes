Mac and Cheese

Ultra creamy and mouth watering mac and cheese that you can't put down. This is the perfect comfort food to share with your family.

  • Elbow Pasta
  • 1/2 lb Shredded Medium Cheddar
  • 1 lb Shredded Mozzerela Cheese
  • 1 lb Shredded Colby Jack Cheese
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Bring a large sauce pan to medium heat and add in your butter
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add in your pasta and cook till el dente
  3. Once the butter has metled add in your flour and salt and pepper to taste and mix for 2 min
  4. Stir in the heavy cream slowly
  5. Once the heavy cream is incorportated add in the cheese with only small handfulls at a time and not adding in more cheese until it has fully melted.
  6. Add in half of the pasta to a cassserole dish and pour on half of the cheese mixture on top
  7. Add in the last half of pasta and the rest of the cheese mixture
  8. Place in the oven on 350 degrees for 40 minutes.
  9. Plate and serve
